My name in full is Ivan Annan; I am a Ghanaian by birth. I am a hardworking and self-motivated person who is open to learning new things and becoming better each day. I am goal-oriented and passion-driven in my daily endeavours.

Web Development Journey

I am a self-taught Front End Developer who had to teach myself how to code despite the fact that I was completely new at the subject. My journey begun when I came across an online course on FutureLearn about Web Development by chance in the latter part of 2019. After finishing the course, I became extraordinarily interested in the subject. I found the whole experience both fun and challenging especially as I had no prior programming knowledge, and I wanted to learn more about the subject. That was when I begun taking more courses, reading more and coding more. Since then, my passion for it has been on the increase especially as I move deeper and deeper into the field.

Currently, I am enthusiastic about web development and daily inspired to learn more and become better. When it comes to coding, I'm always attentive to best practices such as writing clean code and accessibility of webpages and hence employ them when I write code.
